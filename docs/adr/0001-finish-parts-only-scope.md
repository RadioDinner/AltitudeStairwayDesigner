# Package scope is finish parts only; structural parts are out of scope

A Stairway Package contains only *finish* parts (treads, risers, balusters,
handrail, newel posts). Stringers and other structural members are excluded
entirely — not modeled in the 3D view, not configurable, and never on the
purchase order.

Why: stair-parts sellers overwhelmingly fulfill finish components, while
stringers are cut on-site from framing lumber. Scoping to finish parts keeps us
aligned with what a seller can actually fulfill and keeps structural engineering
and liability off our plate.

Note: not rendering stringers means treads/risers need some other visual anchor
in the 3D model (e.g. a closed skirt or the risers themselves) so the staircase
doesn't appear to float — a downstream 3D concern, not a scope change.
